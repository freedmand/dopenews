About Dope News




DopeNews® is an aggregate news website for all news related to doping in sports. This practice includes but is not limited to anabolic steroids, Benzedrine, blood doping, Tylenol™, chocolate milk, horse sperm, endorphines, and selzter water.



Dope News was founded in 2004, and ever since its inception it has been a vibrant hub and community for dope users and nonusers alike to learn more about the current events related to doping in sports, as well as locations to meet up with fellow users and grow as an individual.



Since 2008 the Federal Bureau of Investigation has been surveilling Dope News for supposed allegations of drug trafficking. These accusations are blatantly false, and Dope News and subsidiaries take no responsibility for any illicit drugs users may be able to obtain by reading the first letter of each sentence within articles.
